Chow Mein

Description

Chow mein with veggies, egg, and meat of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of noodles
  • 1 cup of mixed veggies
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of meat
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of oil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Scramble the egg
  3. Cook the meat
  4. Stir fry the veggies
  5. Mix everything together
  6. Enjoy!
